How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Follow this quick and easy guide to make delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

IngredientQuantity
Eggs2
Butter1 tbsp
SaltTo taste

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and stir gently.
  4. Cook until soft and creamy. Season with salt.

Tips

  • Use a non-stick pan for best results.
  • Don't overcook the eggs.
  • Optional: Add Butter or herbs for extra flavor.
